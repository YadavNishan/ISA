Math Geeks

By Raphel Rosen

  • Do you dream about long division in your sleep? Does the thought of solving abstruse equations bring a smile to your face? Do you love celebrating pi every March? Then, Math Geek was made for you! With this guide, you'll learn even more about the power of numbers as you explore their brilliant nature in ways you've never imagined. From manhole covers to bubbles to subway maps, each page gives you a glimpse of the world through renowned mathematicians' eyes and reveals how their theorems and equations can be applied to nearly everything you encounter.

RichDad PoorDad

By Robert T. Kiyosaki

  • Rich Dad Poor Dad is a 1997 book written by Robert T. Kiyosaki and Sharon Lechter. It advocates the importance of financial literacy (financial education), financial independence and building wealth through investing in assets, real estate investing, starting and owning businesses, as well as increasing one's financial intelligence (financial IQ). Rich Dad Poor Dad is written in the style of a set of parables, ostensibly based on Kiyosaki's life.[1] The titular "rich dad" is his friend's father who accumulated wealth due to entrepreneurship and savvy investing, while the "poor dad" is claimed to be Kiyosaki's own father who he says worked hard all his life but never obtained financial security.

Services

Editorial services



Editorial services can include a variety of tasks, depending on the needs of the author and the manuscript. Copyediting involves correcting grammar, spelling, and punctuation errors, while developmental editing focuses on the overall structure and content of the manuscript. Proofreading involves reviewing the final version of the manuscript before it goes to print, to catch any last-minute errors.



Designing



Book publishing companies can provide design and formatting services to create a professional-looking book. This can include creating a visually appealing book cover that will catch the eye of potential readers. The layout and formatting of the text can also be important, as it affects the readability of the book.



Production



Once the manuscript is edited, designed, and formatted, it can be printed and produced. The printing process can vary depending on the publisher, but usually involves creating a master copy of the book and then printing copies as needed. Once the book is printed, it can be bound and packaged for distribution.



Marketing



Marketing and promotion are important services provided by book publishing companies to get the word out about the book. This can include developing a marketing plan, creating promotional materials such as book trailers or social media ads, and organizing events such as book signings or author interviews.



Distribution and sales



Book publishing companies can handle the distribution and sales of the book, getting it into bookstores and online retailers. This can include negotiating deals with distributors and managing inventory levels.



Legal services



Book publishing companies can provide copyright and legal services to help protect the author's intellectual property. This can include securing copyright permissions, handling legal contracts such as publishing agreements, and providing advice on copyright law.
